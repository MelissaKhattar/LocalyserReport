3. Improve Responses

Establish a consistent process for responding to feedback quickly and effectively.

  • Assign Champion: One person per store is responsible.
  • Check Daily: Review new feedback every day.
  • Respond Fast: Aim for 24-48 hour response time.
  • Use Templates: Respond quickly with pre-built templates.
